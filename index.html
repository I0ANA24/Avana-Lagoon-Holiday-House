ABOUT US

Overlooking motus (islands) and water, the Avana Lagoon House is situated at the start of the lagoon that leads to the popular Muri Lagoon. Jump on the canoe and paddle there or walk and snorkel your way down.

Enjoy open space in your two-storey beach house. There are 3 bedrooms (2 with ensuites), with expansive decks on both levels looking out to the views. Ideal for families, the Avana Lagoon House offers affordable accommodation with some of the best views in the South Pacific.
